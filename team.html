Meet the Team!

The original RIT Imaging Team

Our new members (formerly RIT PodSquad)



Initially, our team consisted almost exclusively of Imaging Science and Motion Picture Science sophomores. Members of this team were a part of the 2014-2015 Freshman Imaging Project at RIT during which we successfully researched and built a transient imaging system. The output of the system was a ‘slow-motion’ visualization of a pulse of laser light’s interaction with a scene. Over the course of this project, we forged ourselves into an extraordinarily effective team with an extensive range of talents and skillsets. These assets played a major role in our win of the Special Innovation Award at Design Weekend, and they will continue to play a role in our development of technology for our partners and for the Hyperloop.

After Design Weekend, our team realized the need to expand our represented skill sets even further. The RIT Pod Squad also attended Design Weekend to present their unique pod design, and, due to their team’s expertise in mechanical and electrical systems and knowledge of the project, we decided it would be mutually beneficial for their members to assimilate into the RIT Imaging Team. These new members have been invaluable to the development and production of our current projects, and together we will continue to work towards innovative solutions for the Hyperloop while embodying the core values and work ethic instilled in us by our Alma Mater, Rochester Institute of Technology.
